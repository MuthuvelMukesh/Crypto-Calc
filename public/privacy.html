1. Introduction

Welcome to Indian Crypto Tax & Investment Planner ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

2. Data Collection

Our application is designed with privacy in mind:

  • Local Storage Only: All your data (transactions, preferences, calculations) is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. We do not collect or transmit this data to any server.
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information.
  • Analytics: We may use Google Analytics to understand general usage patterns (page views, session duration). This is anonymized and helps us improve the application.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • CoinGecko API: For cryptocurrency price data. Please review CoinGecko's Privacy Policy.
  • Binance API: For alternative price data. Please review Binance's Privacy Policy.
  • Open Exchange Rates: For USD/INR rates. Please review their privacy policy.
  • Google AdSense: For displaying ads (if enabled). Google may use cookies for personalized advertising. You can opt out via Google Ad Settings.

4. Cookies

We use browser localStorage (not cookies) to save your preferences and transaction data locally. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you are responsible for the security of your device. We recommend:

  • Using strong device passwords
  • Keeping your browser updated
  • Not using shared/public computers for sensitive calculations

6. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the application
  • Export: You can export your transaction data as CSV
  • Delete: Use the "Clear Portfolio" button or clear your browser's localStorage to delete all data

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top will be revised accordingly.

9. Contact Us

10. Disclaimer
